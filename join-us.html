Create your own AI Saturdays

Can't find your city? Become an organizer of AI Saturdays in your city just completing our Onboarding process.

Start Now!

1º Fill out the form

We need to record a few facts first. You will automatically receive an email when you submit this form with access to our quick onboarding course where you will have to introduce yourself. We will contact you ;)

2º Complete the welcome course

In this short course you will learn the basic information about this project, who we are, and you will get to know us so that we can know a little more about how to help you.

3º 1-to-1 meeting

The region leader will contact you to meet us. We will help you in the whole process from the formation of a team, the venue, the learning material and the tools to use.
